🏥 Curriculum Health Index (CHI)
The Curriculum Health Index is a weighted composite score per program. Weighting: 30% Knowledge Retention · 30% Trainer Delivery & Confidence · 40% Post-Training Business Impact. Higher scores indicate a healthier curriculum with stronger real-world performance outcomes.
